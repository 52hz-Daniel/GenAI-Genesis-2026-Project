# Demo script: Aptitude AI for investors, judges, and users

Use this for pitches, hackathon demos, and user walkthroughs. It highlights **Feasible** (technical), **Desirable** (customer), and **Viable** (business).

---

## Talking points (30 seconds)

- **Feasible:** We run a lightweight pipeline (scouts + central Brain) that ingests from allowed sources only, matches opportunities to your profile using your mock-interview history, and surfaces a short feed with a **Confidence Dossier** for each item—why you’re ready, where you’ll struggle, and a Socratic prompt so you decide. No per-user agents; one Brain serves everyone.
- **Desirable:** Students miss deadlines and don’t know where to look. We don’t dump links: we show deadlines (opens/closes), personalize by fit, and for every recommendation we show evidence from *their* practice and an honest blind spot. “We remember you and help you decide.”
- **Viable:** B2C free; retention through personalization and deadline awareness. We don’t scrape LinkedIn or X; we use public APIs, RSS, and curated sources so the model stays sustainable.

---

## Step-by-step demo flow (about 2 minutes)

1. **Sign in**  
   Use Google (or Microsoft/Apple if configured). Mention that we support multiple sign-in options for students and schools.

2. **Do one short mock interview**  
   Go to **Interview**, answer one behavioral question, and finish or complete the session. This gives the platform memory (weakness, strength, competencies) so the next step is personalized.

3. **Open Opportunities**  
   In the nav, click **Opportunities**. You should see a short list (up to 5) of opportunities or trends matched to the user.

4. **Open one opportunity**  
   Click **“See why you’re ready”** on one card. The **Confidence Dossier** modal opens with:
   - **Why you’re ready** (Competency Bridge — uses evidence from their mock interview)
   - **Watch out for** (Blind Spot — honest gap if relevant)
   - **Reflection** (Socratic prompt — they decide, we don’t)

5. **Take an action**  
   Click **I’ll apply**, **Save for later**, or **Not for me**. This shows we optimize for action, not infinite scroll.

6. **Optional**  
   - Switch to the **Trends** tab (if available) to show cutting-edge content.  
   - Point out **deadline badges** (“Closes in X days” / “Opens on …”) to reinforce “we help you not miss deadlines.”

---

## One-liner for the product

“Aptitude AI is the anti-LinkedIn for students: we don’t dump links. We use your practice history to show you why you’re ready, where you’ll struggle, and help you decide—so you don’t miss deadlines and you act with confidence.”
