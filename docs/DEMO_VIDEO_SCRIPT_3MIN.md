# Aptitude AI: 3-Minute Demo Video Script

*For AI-generated video with voiceover. Use this script for the voice track; insert screen recordings at the marked segments and use Veo prompts for AI-generated visuals.*

---

## Constraints

- **Length:** ~3 minutes total (~450–500 words at natural pace).
- **Judging criteria:** Equal weight to all four: Innovation & Originality, Technical Complexity & Execution, Product Experience & Design, Impact & Practical Value.
- **Tone:** High-energy, passionate, confident.
- **Markers:** `[SCREEN RECORDING]` = insert your own product screen capture at that point.
- **Veo prompts:** One per ~10 seconds of script (≈18 prompts); visual arc from "Interview Anxiety" (glitchy, darker) to "Career Mastery" (fluid, luminous, data-driven).
- **Content:** Include Feasible / Desirable / Viable, UN SDG 4 & 8, Best AI for Community Impact + Best Education AI Hack; reference only features actually built.

**Timing guide:** Each block below is ~10 seconds of speech (~25–30 words). Numbers in brackets are approximate script seconds for Veo placement.

---

## Implementation Notes

### Screen recordings

Record 3–4 clips and drop them in at the `[SCREEN RECORDING]` markers; trim to fit the ~10s blocks if needed:

1. **Clip 1:** Sign in with Google → one short mock interview (behavioral questions, optional voice) → finish session → clear feedback and session focus → badge unlock.
2. **Clip 2:** Translate page: paste an experience → get ATS-friendly, NACE-aligned resume bullets. Optionally show live voice interview: start and ~30s of conversation.
3. **Clip 3:** Opportunities: open feed → click one card → open Confidence Dossier → show I'll apply / Save for later / Not for me.
4. **Clip 4 (recap):** Quick flow across Translate, Mock interview + badge, Live interview, Opportunities + dossier.

### Veo

- Use the numbered Veo prompts as-is or adjust for your AI video tool's style.
- Keep the arc: **anxiety/dark/glitch (start)** → **memory/technical (middle)** → **clarity/dossier/impact (second half)** → **mastery/community (end)**.

### Numbers

If you re-estimate with Python (e.g. CAC, conversion, institutional deal size), add one sentence in the "viable" or "impact" block, e.g. "We're built for scalable unit economics and clear paths to institutional revenue."

### Citations

If you later identify any code adapted from a public repo, add one line in the script or in [INVESTOR_JUDGE_REPORT.md](INVESTOR_JUDGE_REPORT.md) and keep the script at 3 minutes by trimming elsewhere (e.g. shorten the recap or the optional extension).
