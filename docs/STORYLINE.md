# Aptitude AI: Storyline and Messaging

Single source for UX and marketing copy. Use this for the landing hero, metadata, and investor pitch.

## Headline

**Resume bullets + interview practice that remember you.**

(Alternative: *Your career companion that gets better the more you use it.*)

## Subline

Built for students. No judgment, no sign-up wall. Your first win is under 60 seconds away.

## Target user

- University students (especially sophomores, juniors)
- First-generation and anxious achievers
- People who want resume bullets and behavioral interview practice without generic tools

## Differentiators (3 bullets)

1. **Memory across sessions.** We build on your progress. The coach knows your past feedback and weaknesses.
2. **Role-specific practice.** Paste a job description and get tailored questions and feedback for that role.
3. **Elite feedback.** Pyramid, MECE, STAR with quantifiable results (not “good job”). Framework-based coaching.

## Proof points

- First win in under 60 seconds (no sign-up required).
- Remembers your weaknesses and re-tests you.
- Role-specific practice when you add a job description.
- Digital badges you can share (e.g. LinkedIn).

- Landing hero and “How we’re different” strip
- App metadata (title, description, Open Graph, Twitter)
- Profile and progress copy
- Investor-facing one-pager or deck (align wording with this doc)
